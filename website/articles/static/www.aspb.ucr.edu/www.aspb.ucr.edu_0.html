About Us

Since 1986, the Associated Students Program Board (ASPB) has been bringing quality campus entertainment, including Concerts, Free Film Premieres, Speaking Engagements, Contemporary Culture and Special events to the UC Riverside campus and community. Each year, ASPB brings bigger and better events to cultivate the diverse UCR student body. In 2016, ASPB celebrates it's 30th Anniversary on the UC Riverside campus providing students with top-notch entertainment.

Get Involved!

ASPB has many opportunities through out the year to get invovled. You can apply to be part of the ASPB team during Fall, Winter and Spring quarters (Positions vary depending on the quarter). ASPB offers hand on, practical experience that rivals many off-campus internships. In addition to great work experience, ASPB members receive valuable professional and leadership development throughout the year. To learn more about how you can get involved with ASPB email us at aspb@ucr.edu.
