Invest in your Success

Highlander Early Start Academy (HESA) will challenge you, and attending will give you more confidence, provide you with knowledge about the campus, and help you feel at home at UC Riverside.

Benefits of Highlander Early Start Academy

By participating in the program, students will:

  • Receive a $500.00 scholarship towards the HESA program tuition and fees. An additional $250.00 scholarship will be awarded to particpants who earn a 2.5 GPA during the summer.
  • Receive an additional $250.00 in the Fall, Winter and Spring quarters for each quarter that you enroll in 15 units and maintain a 2.5 GPA.
  • Receive 8 (Math 6A Academy) or 9 units (English 4 and Math 5 Academy) as well as fulfill a graduation requirement course (History).
      • Be College Level English ready by fall
      • Be Calculus ready by fall
    • Special academic & personal success support designed especially for Early Start students.
    • Gain an edge over other incoming freshmen with early access to advising, campus life and resources.
    • Be automatically enrolled in their college's freshmen Learning Community.

The University of California, Riverside (www.ucr.edu) is a doctoral research university, a living laboratory for groundbreaking exploration of issues critical to Inland Southern California, the state and communities around the world. Reflecting California's diverse culture, UCR’s enrollment has exceeded 21,000 students. The campus opened a medical school in 2013 and has reached the heart of the Coachella Valley by way of the UCR Palm Desert Center. The campus has an annual statewide economic impact of more than $1 billion.
