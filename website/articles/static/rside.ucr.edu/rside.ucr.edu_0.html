UCR Fun Facts

It’s a fact – UC Riverside has plenty of things to brag about. Check out a few of them. It’s not just trivia – it’s part of who we R!

The official UC Riverside Tartan isn’t just any old plaid. It’s a symbol of Highlander history & Tartan Soul. Created by Linda Clifford and members of the UCR Pipe Band, it’s even registered with the proper authorities in Scotland!
