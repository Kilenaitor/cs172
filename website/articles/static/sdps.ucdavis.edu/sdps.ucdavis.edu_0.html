Staff Development and Professional Services fosters and supports your learning and performance by providing innovative, high-quality programs, resources and services. We offer staff a wide array of courses, programs and certificate series on nearly 300 topics, as well as confidential career counseling, a resource library, online toolkits and management consultation regarding learning and performance support.

Career Management Academy

“If you don’t know where you are going, you will probably end up somewhere else” (Laurence J. Peter). The Career Management Academy offers career assessments that facilitate self-knowledge, the development of professional goals and increased job satisfaction. In this course you will inventory your work values, transferable skills, personality, interests and skills, which are integral steps in developing career goals.

Visit the Career Management Academy course listing for additional details and a link to register.
