Transfer Admission

UC San Diego looks for transfer students at the junior-year level who are well-prepared to succeed in a rigorous and challenging academic setting. Admission is competitive and you must exceed the minimum requirements. Each year about 30% of the new students on the UC San Diego campus are transfer students.
