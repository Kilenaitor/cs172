UCR's undergraduate student body is among the 5 most diverse of major research universities in the United States, with graduation rates above the national average for all students and African American students leading the way.

UCR is a Department of Education recognized HSI that is both a Carnegie top category research and U.S. News Tier One institution.

Campus Pride lists UCR among the nation's best for lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender students--one of the 19 given the 5 star rating. UCR is also featured in The Advocate College Guide for LGBT students.

MEI offers diversified education and training for staff and faculty with more than 20 new seminars and workshops.

The University of California, Riverside (www.ucr.edu) is a doctoral research university, a living laboratory for groundbreaking exploration of issues critical to Inland Southern California, the state and communities around the world. Reflecting California's diverse culture, UCR’s enrollment has exceeded 21,000 students. The campus opened a medical school in 2013 and has reached the heart of the Coachella Valley by way of the UCR Palm Desert Center. The campus has an annual statewide economic impact of more than $1 billion.
