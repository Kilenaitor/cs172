Making a Difference

Whatever your philanthropic passion may be—whether it is improving energy-efficient power, feeding the world, curing cancer or helping a student in need—you can find a way to support it by giving to UC Davis.

We are ranked one of the top 10 public universities in the country and the No. 1 agricultural school in the world. We are looking for visionaries who want to help us find innovative solutions to the world’s most critical challenges, provide top-level care for patients, and ensure that deserving students receive a top-quality education.

Thank you for considering a gift to UC Davis. Discover your philanthropic passion here.
