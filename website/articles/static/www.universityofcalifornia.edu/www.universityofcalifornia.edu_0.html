Regents meet under new Board meeting structure

At the September meeting, held at UCLA's Luskin Conference Center, the Regents met under a new structure which included reconstituted Committees established with the adoption in July of new Bylaws. The format included concurrent Committee meetings on day 1, followed by a meeting of the full Board on day 2, allowing for in-depth discussions on various topics.
