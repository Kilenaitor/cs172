"Welcome. As chancellor of UC San Francisco, I have both the opportunity and the obligation to provide forward-looking leadership and to ensure that UCSF remains a model of excellence for the country at a time of great opportunity in the health sciences. Not only does UCSF have a tremendous impact locally, but the work that we do also makes a significant difference both nationally and internationally. Our discovery is critical to driving the future of health care. Learn more about the talented professionals who directly support me toward this goal, as well as the exciting and cutting-edge initiatives under way at UCSF." — Sam Hawgood, MBBS
