Welcome

  • PDF

Welcome to the website of the Postdoctoral Scholars Association (PSA) of the University of California - San Francisco (UCSF).

UCSF is home to approximately 1,100 Postdocs and they are spread over many different locations throughout the city. To connect Postdocs and create a sense of community PSA has set its mission to:

  • To develop a sense of community by fostering professional and social interactions among postdoctoral scholars throughout the institution.
  • To provide a forum to disseminate accurate information and address issues relevant to postdoctoral scholars. 
  • To promote a productive dialogue among postdoctoral scholars, the administration, and the faculty.
