Your contacts for the UC Davis TAG

Each California community college identifies one representative—your community college contact—to serve as the primary contact for UC Davis Transfer Admission Guarantees and to work closely with their UC Davis contact. If your community college contact shown below has changed, please notify Cindy Bevc at 530-754-7649 or cabevc@ucdavis.edu.

Each California community college also identifies one or more UC TAG administrators (ADMIN) who will authorize representatives from your college to submit UC TAGs. If you are an authorized UC TAG administrator, please update your UC Davis TAG Reviewer assignments by logging into your counselor account.
