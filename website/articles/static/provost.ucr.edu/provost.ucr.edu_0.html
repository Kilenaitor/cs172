The University of California at Riverside (UCR) is implementing a major expansion of our faculty and investing in state-of-the-art research facilities to support their work.Â This expansion will build critical mass in 34 vital and emerging fields of scholarship, foster truly cross-disciplinary work, and further diversify the faculty at one of America's most diverse research universities.

UCR is home to some of the most productive faculty in the world. They are at the core of a campus community that achieves success through access rather than exclusion. Student surveys consistently show that UCR is a national leader in creating a campus climate where underrepresented minority students and lower-income students feel respected and welcomed. This inclusive environment has led to some of the lowest racial and socioeconomic graduation rate gaps in the country.

Why Ucr? Diversity, Excellence, Community

UCR embodies how diversity can foster truly innovative research, creative curricula, and a healthy, engaging, and vibrant campus climate. We encourage applications from scholars committed to this mission. Join us in defining the new American research university.
