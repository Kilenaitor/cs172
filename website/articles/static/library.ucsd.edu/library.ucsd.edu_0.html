3D CAVEkiosk Now Open in Geisel Library

Travel to at-risk cultural heritage sites around the world and explore them in virtual reality at Geisel Library’s new 3D CAVEkiosk. The life-size kiosk, located in the East Commons on the main floor, is open to the public during Library open hours.

UC San Diego's Pulitzer Prize-Winning Composer

Roger Reynolds is a pioneer in the use of new technologies for musical purposes. Explore this collection featuring his compositional sketches and diagrams, films, interviews, and photos from the 1968 and 2011 multi-media performances of his innovative piece, PING.
