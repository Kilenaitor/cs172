thursdaynightlive.ucr.edu Thursday Nights Live

Thursday Nights Live are musical events that bring faculty, staff, and graduate students together to enjoy each other's company and to listen to live musical sets performed by Southern California musicians.  Thursday Nights Live take place in the HUB Plaza from 5:15-8:15 p.m.  Wine, beer, soft drinks, and food are available for purchase.  Parking is free in Lot 1 for the first 50 cars.

We are excited to have Qutapenas performing on November 17.  We will also have some new food choices beginning November 17.  Beer is still $3, and wine only $4.

Thanks to the tremendous support of UCR faculty, staff, and graduate students, TNL has been funded for the rest of the academic year.  We expect to hold six events each term.   Parking for the first 50 cars is free in Lot 1. TNL is rain or shine! If it rains, TNL will move to the HUB Dining Area.

The schedule for the remainder of fall term is as follows:

Nov. 17: Quitapenas

Dec. 1: Better Late

Dec. 8: Brothers Igniting a Groove

The campus is also sponsoring a farmer's market on Thursday nights from 4:00-8:00 p.m. at Glen Mor Market.  You may want to check it out before or after!  

The TNL Advisory Committee consists of Ken Barish (Physics), Steve Brint (Sociology & Public Policy), Javier Hurtado (Theater), Yolanda Moses (Anthropology), Louis Van Den Berg (KUCR), Ameae Walker (Vice Provost of Academic Affairs and Biomedical Sciences), and Georgia Warnke (Center for Ideas & Society and Political Science). Assistant Vice Chancellor Brenda Ice oversees administrative support.

Please fill out the 5-question survey at the following link to tell us about your experience and to suggest musicians for spring term:
