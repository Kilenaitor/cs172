Xin Ge Received NIH R01 Grant for Antibody Research

CEE PHD Student, Peter Byrley, Wins 2016 UC-wide GradSlam

CEE Graduate Student Video Receives Honorable Mention from AEESP 2015-16 Student Video Competition

“The Salton Sea: Obstacles and Opportunities in Environmental Engineering and Science” video was awarded honorable mention in 2015-16 Student Video Competition from the Association of Environmental Engineering and Science Professors (AEESP).
 

Two CEE undergrads, Jose and Shilpan, won the national competition for mobile app design at the AIChE conference. Undergraduate student teams created an APP for a mobile device such as a smartphone (iPhone Android, Blackberry, etc). The award includes individual certificates and a monetary prize for the Student Chapter. 
 

CEE PHD Student, Gongde Chen, Won 3rd Place for Best Student Poster Award at the Emerging Contaminant Summit in Denver, Colorado
 

Three CEE undergraduate teams all won $10,000 grants from Southern California World Water Forum College Grant Program.

Our teams will work on “Recovery of Inorganic Phosphorus from Membrane Antiscalant in Reverse Osmosis Concentrate”, “NiTreat:  A Sustainable Approach to Nitrate Removal from Drinking Water Resource” and “Sustain-a-Drain: An Advanced Storm Drain Indicative Maintenance Process”.

CEE undergraduate team “Titania Coatings” won 2016 WERC / IEE International Environmental Design Contest. Our multidisciplinary team tied for the Second Place Award for Task 1 and tied for First Place for the Peer Review Award for a total monetary award of $1000.

 

UCR Undergraduate Husk-to-Home and Sustain-a-Dream Teams Respectively Won 1st and 2nd Place Awards at the 2016 Spring Green Expo/Eco Innovators Showcase in Los Angeles
