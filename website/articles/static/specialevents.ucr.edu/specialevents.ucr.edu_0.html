About the Office of Event Management & Protocol

The Office of Event Management & Protocol supports the university mission of teaching, research, and service through the effective development and management of events that communicate and enhance UCR's reputation for excellence, especially events specific to the advancement of the university.

The goals of the office are to:

  • Serve in the planning, development and execution of high-profile major events that benefit the university and enhance its image, reputation and attractiveness as worthy focus of charitable giving and other forms of support.
  • Partner with various academic and administrative units on campus in order to achieve their individual event goals and objectives.
  • Advise members of the UCR community on protocol issues as they relate to event planning.

Upcoming Events

New Student Convocation - September 20, 2016 

To mark the beginning of a student's journey as a member of the UCR community, New Student Convocation is held each fall for the entering class. At this formal academic ceremony, leaders in the University community welcome all incoming students into the Highlander family, and reaffirm the core values of the university - free inquiry, intellectual honesty, personal integrity and respect for human dignity.

Homecoming - November 19, 2016

Homecoming weekend at UCR is the highlight of UCR's fall quarter. Events include: Alumni reunions, bonfire, Scot Fest and the Basketball Game. All are welcome - Go Highlanders!

Hays Press-Enterprise Lecture  - TBD

The Hays Press-Enterprise Lecture has been presented by The Press-Enterprise and UCR annually since 1966. The goal of this free public lecture is to bring someone of exceptional achievement in journalism to Riverside to address important topics related to the news media.

Donors & Scholars Recognition Luncheon  - May 20, 2017

This annual event brings together the generous scholarship donors and talented scholarship recipients to celebrate achievement and philanthropy.

Commencement  - June 16-19, 2017

UCR's 63rd Commencement is scheduled for June 2017, beginning on Friday, June 16 and concluding Monday, June 19 on beautiful Pierce Lawn.

The University of California, Riverside (www.ucr.edu) is a doctoral research university, a living laboratory for groundbreaking exploration of issues critical to Inland Southern California, the state and communities around the world. Reflecting California's diverse culture, UCR’s enrollment has exceeded 21,000 students. The campus opened a medical school in 2013 and has reached the heart of the Coachella Valley by way of the UCR Palm Desert Center. The campus has an annual statewide economic impact of more than $1 billion.
