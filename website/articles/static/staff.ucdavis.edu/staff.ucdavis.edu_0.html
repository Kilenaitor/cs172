Wanted...Bakers & Tasters!

Staff Assembly presents its December 12 Holiday Bake-Off: Bars Edition! The bake-off, now in its second year, is for bakers and tasters! Please enter by December 7.

Bakers will bring their bars to the bake-off for the people's tasting! Bakers register here.

If you’re not a baker, you can sign up to be a taster. Tasters register here.

The tasters will choose their favorites, and the top five bars will go to the three judges, who will choose the winners.
