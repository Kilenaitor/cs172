Celebrate UCR's 62nd Commencement

Welcome to the official Web site for UC Riverside's 62nd Commencement to be held June 10–13, 2016.

If you are a student about to graduate, congratulations on your achievement – you have worked hard to get to this point of your life!

Making this day a memorable occasion for you and your proud family members and friends is very important to UC Riverside, and we've developed this Web site to provide you with information on the ceremonies, what to expect, where to go, and what to do. Additional details will be posted to this site as they become available, so please check back periodically.

Students who expect to graduate Fall 2015, Winter 2016 or Spring 2016 are eligible to participate in Commencement 2016. Graduation application deadlines and additional information can be found on the following web sites:

Bachelor's Degree Candidates

Bourns College of Engineering (BCOE)

College of Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences (CHASS)

College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences (CNAS)

School of Business Administration (SOBA)

Advanced Degree Candidates

Specific eligibility information and application deadlines can be found on the Graduate Division website.

Teacher Credential Candidates

For specific eligibility information and application deadlines contact your Student Affairs Officer.
