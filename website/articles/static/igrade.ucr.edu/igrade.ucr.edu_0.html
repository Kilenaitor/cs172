Welcome

iGrade is an automated grade-submission system for UCR faculty. Using iGrade, UCR faculty submit their course grades directly and electronically to the University Registrar. While grades may be entered into iGrade at any time, the availability to submit grades using the iGrade system is dependent on the availability of Student Information System.
