              Policies for a Positive Campus

              UCR students must follow all student conduct policies. Take the time to read and understand them. Following policies helps ensure a safe campus community where everyone can work, live and learn without interference.

              • Read the Standards of Conduct
                View the main rules that guide acceptable behavior at UCR.
              • See Academic Integrity Policies and Procedures
                Find out what is expected of students when it comes to academics and ethics.
              • Learn the Good Neighbor Guidelines
                Understand how students should behave as members of our shared Riverside community.
              • Report Sexual Assault
                Get help for yourself and others. Learn the standard procedure for reporting sexual assault on campus.
              • View Other Campus Policies
                Discover additional policies connected to technology, tobacco and more.
              • Contact Us
                Get in touch if you want to learn more about policies and procedures associated with student conduct at UCR.
