C4's mission is to improve scholarly and applied knowledge on community colleges, and aid practitioners in improving community colleges by conducting research, providing services to institutions, and by preparing graduate students as researchers. 


C4's policy research and organizational development agenda will help prepare faculty and administrators to be leaders in shaping their own institutions and higher education in California.
