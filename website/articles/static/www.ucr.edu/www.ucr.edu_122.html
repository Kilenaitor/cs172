Blue and Gold Opportunity Fund

UC's new Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan will cover your systemwide fees if you are a California resident whose family earns less than $80,000 a year and you qualify for financial aid.

R'Web: Your Personal Web Portal. Log onto R'Web and access Growl, iLearn, Webmail and Facebook all in one place!

Supporting Your Needs as You Shape Your Future

There's more to college life than the classroom. It’s also about the quality of your daily life and preparing you for life after college. UCR has services and resources that can support you every step of the way, whether it is deciding where to eat between classes or making plans for your future.

Campus Safety

General information on Campus Police, Campus Safety Escort Service, the Campus Emergency Notification System and Environmental Health and Safety. 

Career Center

UCR’s Career Center provides students and alumni with access to an extensive job database, career counseling, offer resume and interview advice and alert you to upcoming workshops and job fairs. 

Child Development Center

Our Child Development Center provides a safe, nurturing environment for the children of UCR students, staff and faculty. The center has programs for children ages four months to kindergarten age.

Dining Services

UCR Dining Services provides the campus with great food as well as a variety of locations to relax, socialize or study. From the dining halls to our retail locations, we offer high-quality and healthy dining options in a friendly, welcoming environment.

Ethnic & Gender Program Offices

The Ethnic and Gender Program Offices feature student support services, community involvement, multi-cultural events and leadership training that allows students to engage in cultural growth and development and build the skills needed to lead and succeed in a diverse world.

Health, Fitness and Wellbeing

UCR is an environment for students, staff and faculty that promotes a vibrant, healthy and balanced lifestyle. We have several integrated wellness programs that foster a culture of health and wellness and promote the importance of maintaining an optimal quality of life.

Housing Services

UCR Housing Services provides our students with safe, convenient, student-oriented housing. We offer three housing styles — residence halls, campus apartments and family housing — to meet the needs of all our students. Each provides the opportunity to participate in activities that enrich your college experience. Our on-call housing staff, supported by the campus police force, are committed to providing a safe and secure environment.

International Resources

The International Resources website features a variety of links for students seeking information on international programs here at UCR as well as other opportunities around the globe.

R'Card

Your R'Card is your single most important piece of student identification.

This multi-purpose ID allows you to:

  • Verify your identity on campus and in the community.
  • Pay for meals.
  • Borrow books from the library.
  • Gain access to the Student Recreation Center (SRC).
  • Get into UCR home athletic events.
  • Gain access to some campus buildings.
  • Make purchases on campus using  "Bear Bucks."
  • Use vending machines, laundry services, and copy machines.
  • Ride the Riverside Transit Agency bus for free.
  • Use registrar services.

Student Special Services

Student Special Services helps to create an accessible university environment for all students. 

Resources include:

  • Services for students with disabilities.
  • Veterans' educational benefits.
  • Voter registration.
  • ROTC referral.
  • Selective Service information.

UCR Campus Store

The UCR Campus Store is your best choice for textbooks and UCR merchandise. You’ll find the complete booklist for all your classes, as well as the largest selection of UCR apparel, school spirit items and gifts. CompUCR features electronics, computers, software and supplies.
