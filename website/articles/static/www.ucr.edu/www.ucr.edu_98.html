Who Gives to UC Riverside?

Every year, thousands of people make gifts to UCR. Their reasons for giving are as diverse as the donors themselves:

  • Grateful alumni looking to give back.
  • Parents of students honoring their children's education.
  • Staff, faculty, friends and neighbors who believe in the university's mission.
  • Corporations and foundations who want to make a difference in students' lives.

Who are these donors? They are people just like you who have chosen to make a difference in the lives of others.

Lelia and Mir Mulla

The Mullas recently established the Mir S. Mulla and Lelia L. Mulla Endowed Scholarship Fund. Students with high scholastic achievement in the College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences will be eligible to receive funding from this endowment.
