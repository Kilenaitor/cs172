Give Now

Living the Promise: The Campaign for UC Riverside. We are living the promise. Join us!

UCR has a long and successful history of partnering with national, regional and family foundations. The Foundation Relations team is available to assist foundation partners with visits to campus, meetings with faculty and key leadership, and other UCR connections

Contact us to request information, schedule a visit, enjoy a customized tour of campus facilities or arrange for an on-site visit with one of our knowledgeable staff. We look forward to discussing your next philanthropic endeavor or strategic partnership.
