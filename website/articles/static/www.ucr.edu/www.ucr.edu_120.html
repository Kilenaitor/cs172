Great People Leading the Way

Led by Chancellor Kim A. Wilcox and a team of dynamic administrators, UCR is on the rise. We have a number of up-and-coming departments, programs, initiatives and professional schools that are breaking new ground and establishing high-quality benchmarks.

Office of the Vice Chancellor for Student Affairs

Overseen by Vice Chancellor for Student Affairs James Sandoval, the Office of Vice Chancellor for Student Affairs is the umbrella organization responsible for all student-oriented departments. Responsibilities include oversight of recreation, student organizations and community service.

Dean of Students

Under the guidance of Assistant Vice Chancellor, Student Affairs and Dean of Students Susan Allen Ortega, the office of the Dean of Students oversees programs, services and activities that promote intellectual, personal, social and ethical development of students beyond the classroom. 

Learn more about our student policies, support services and organizations.

Fees & Financial Aid

The cost to attend UCR depends on if you live at home, on-campus or in off-campus housing. UCR projects an annual budget of educational costs and living expenses to help families prepare financially.

About 70 percent of all UCR students receive some form of financial aid, including:

  • Grants provided by the federal government, the state of California and UCR. Awarded on the basis of financial need.
  • Scholarships awarded on the basis of merit, academic achievement, athletic ability or special talents. Provided by UCR and outside agencies.
  • The Blue and Gold Opportunity Plan which covers your system-wide fees if you are a California resident whose family earns less than $80,000 a year (as of 2011-12) and you qualify for financial aid.
  • Loans provided by the federal government, UCR and private lenders.
  • Work opportunities through the Federal Work Study program, coordinated by UCR’s Career Services Office.

Office of Student Life

Led by Assistant Dean of Students Tonantzin Oseguera, the Office of Student Life oversees a variety of programs, activities and organizations that help create a great environment for UCR students. 

Programs include orientation, the First-Year Success Program, as well as oversight of student organizations including leadership and honor societies.

Registrar

The Office of the Registrar is responsible for student registration, publishes class schedules, maintains academic records and distributes diplomas. It also protects your rights as a student by upholding The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).
