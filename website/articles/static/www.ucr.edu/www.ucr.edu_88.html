UCR Campus Store: Your #1 Source for Everything UCR

The UCR Campus Store is your official store for UCR merchandise and textbooks. It’s the only place where you’ll find the complete booklist for all your classes, as well as the largest selection of UCR apparel, school spirit items and gifts. Check out CompUCR section to shop for electronics, computers, software, and supplies. Stop in or visit us online!
