CSS provides desktop computing support for faculty and staff. Our services include consulting and troubleshooting hardware, software and networking, plus assistance with acquiring, learning and using stand-alone or networked computers or smartphones. We offer telephone support, on-site and carry-in services, on-line remote support, and software downloads. We also implemented and spearhead the Microcomputer Support Specialist (MSS) program, which provides decentralized departmental support.

Mission Statement

CSS's mission is to provide the best possible, timely service to faculty and staff with the ultimate goal of enhancing UCR's teaching and research missions. By assisting faculty and staff in the use and maintenance of their computer hardware and software, we can invigorate the University's instructional dynamic.

Services

  • Visit the CSS Website The Computer Support Services group is dedicated to providing timely, convenient, quality service to UCR faculty and staff. Visit our web site to see the wide variety of services and convenient service packages we offer!
  • Microsoft Office 365 Email for StaffUCR has deployed Microsoft's cloud based email, calendaring, and collaboration tools suite for all UCR staff to securely and easily access UCR e-mail from anywhere in the world, offering synchronization to the Outlook client and Smartphones. 
  • Google R'Mail for FacultyUCR has deployed Google's cloud based email, calendaring, and collaboration tools suite for all UCR faculty and academics. UCR will leverage the Google Apps for Education suite of tools and services. G'mail powered email accounts provide unlimited e-mail storage, server-side virus scanning of all e-mail messages, web-based access, and POP/IMAP access. R'mail provides faculty and academics with a high level of flexibility and security.
  • MSS ProgramThe MSS program was created at UCR over twenty years ago to promote cooperation between the central computing support organization and local IT support representatives at the departmental level. The program serves as a conduit for sharing information and resources helpful in resolving desktop computing related problems.
  • Software Site LicensingComputing & Communications site licensing services provides the ability for UCR faculty, staff and students to purchase software at a substantial discount. In many cases we work with other UC campuses and the Office of the President to obtain the best possible pricing.
