The UCR Alumni Association has realized a long-held objective: completion of an Alumni & Visitors Center for the University of California, Riverside. The Alumni & Visitors Center serves as the “front door” to the campus – a distinctive arrival point that showcases alumni achievements while welcoming friends and visitors. The 13,865-square-foot, split-level building designed by local architects HMC, houses a large lobby, meeting rooms, a library of works by UCR authors, and a formal board room. The facility is available for special events, meetings and receptions.

Are you an alumni artist?

If you are an alumnus who would like to have your art displayed in the Goldware Family Library at the Alumni & Visitors Center please contact us at (951) 827-2586 or ucralum@ucr.edu.

Legacy Brick Program

For a tax-deductible gift of $500, you can purchase a 4×8 inch brick inscribed with a three-line, 20 character name or message of your choice that will be displayed in the entrance courtyard of the Alumni & Visitors Center. Learn more about the Legacy Brick Program.
