UCR Alumni Association Board Nominations: Serve on the  UCRAA 2017 Board of Directors. Help promote the vision, mission, and goals of the association and UCR. Deadline to apply is December 31 for a term start date of July 2017.

Living the Promise Receptions: Alumni and friends are invited to a reception with UCR Chancellor Kim A. Wilcox, campus leadership and faculty. Connect with fellow Highlanders as we explore ways UCR is impacting the community, nation and world, and how YOU are part of it all!

Class of 2016: Join the UCR Alumni Association now to remain connected to your alma mater and receive $100 off of a lifetime membership as well as exclusive graduation gifts!

Legacy Brick Program: Celebrate a milestone or honor a family member! Proceeds from the Legacy Brick program support the Alumni Association Freshman Scholarship endowment, which provides $4,000 scholarships to incoming freshmen.
