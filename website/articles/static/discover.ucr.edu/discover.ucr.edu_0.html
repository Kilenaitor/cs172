Discover Day 2016 FAQs

Do I have to make a reservation?
Yes. Please let us know that you will be attending and how many guests you will be bringing with you. You can provide your reservation through the Reserve link in the top navigation, or by clicking here.
Do I have to show up right at 10:00 a.m. to check in?
While it's not a requirement for you to show up at 10:00 a.m., please keep in mind that the first session will begin promptly at 10:00 a.m. and most sessions are not repeated. Your best bet is to take a look at the agenda for Discover Day and plan your day from there.
Is there a cost to attend?
Nope. It’s on the house.
Is there a charge for Discover Day parking?
No, parking is free.
Where do I park FOr Discover Day?
Parking Lot 30.
Once I park, where do I go?
We’ll have signs directing you. Follow the crowd.
What happens at Discover Day?
Discover Day is a way for you to learn more about the campus and life at UC Riverside. If you are unsure about the major you are interested in applying for, Discover Day is a great opportunity to explore your options. We have presentations from faculty, staff and students, application workshops, information sessions, housing tours, department open houses, lunch at the Highlander Union Building, all day entertainment and much more planned. Download the event agenda, which will have all the event details.
What is the difference between Discover Day and Highlander Day?

Discover Day, held on November 19th, is our largest open house for students interested in applying and attending UC Riverside. Programming includes application workshops, information sessions about the campus, individual college presentations to help you decide which to apply to, campus tours, housing tours and much more.

Highlander Day, held on April 8th 2016, is our largest open house for recently admitted students for the upcoming Fall term. Programming includes next steps to enrollment, resources available, how to get involved on campus, financial aid, campus tours, and housing tours. The day is based around welcoming you into the Highlander family and showing what life at UC Riverside is all about.
What is the Overnight Host Program?
The Overnight Host Program is an opportunity to sign-up to stay overnight on-campus with a UCR student as your host. The overnight stay will be offered the night of Friday, November 18, 2016.
Who can stay overnight?
The Overnight Host Program is for prospective students that are juniors or seniors in high school and are seriously considering UCR for their undergraduate degree.
Does everyone get to stay overnight?
Capacity for hosting is about 100 and accommodations will be granted on a first come first served.
Is there a fee for staying overnight?
No fee for overnight stay. Information will be sent to those who register and are confirmed.
