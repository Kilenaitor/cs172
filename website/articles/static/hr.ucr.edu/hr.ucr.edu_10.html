Benefits, Compensation & Classification Resources

  • At Your Service Online  (UCnet)
  • Employment Service Credit Verification

    Employment service credit will follow an employee transferring from one UC location to another, but this is not an automatic process. Employees are responsible for requesting verification of prior UC, CSU, DOE, and State of California employment service credit to ensure an accurate account of their total employee service credit. Payroll and/or the employee's department are responsible for the verification process.  Please refer to the Employee Service Credit Verification Guidelines, which provides detailed guidance on the verification and transferring of employment service credit. 
  • iReview

    iReview is UCR's online staff equity and reclassification review system. It allows for the completely paperless creation, routing, review and approval of equity and reclassification requests.  For more information on iReview, please visit the iReview support site.

  • Human Resources Data Warehouse (HRDW)

    The Human Resources & Academic Personnel Data Warehouse (HRDW) is an exciting new tool available to the UC Riverside community. HRDW makes the valuable information contained in UC Riverside's Personnel Payroll System (PPS) easily accessible through a powerful web interface.

  • Job Description System (JDS)

    The JDS is UCR's online repository of staff job descriptions. Using the JDS, you can initiate a new job description, search, edit & approve modifications to existing job descriptions. The system is designed to work seamlessly with the iRecruit and iReview systems. For more information on the JDS, please visit the JDS support site.
  • Title and Pay Plan (T&PP)

    The Title and Pay Plan is a comprehensive online system that allows users to search for UCR job titles and title codes and find the corresponding salary grades, step structures, class specifications, bargaining units, overtime eligibility, shift differentials, and on-call rates.
  • SMG Title and Pay Plan
  • Total Compensation Calculator

    The value of your UC employment goes beyond the base salary. UC employees enjoy a full range of benefits, services and programs. The Total Compensation Calculator calculates the approximate value of your total UC compensation, with medical and dental plans included.
