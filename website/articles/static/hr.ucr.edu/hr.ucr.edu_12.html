The UC Riverside Work/Life program is committed to developing dynamic, problem-solving strategies, which assist staff and faculty to successfully balance their personal and professional challenges.  At UC Riverside, work/life resources are divided into three broad categories:

  • Financial Balance
  • Personal Balance
  • Professional Balance

Wellness Program

UC Riverside cares about the health of its entire campus community! A variety of wellness programs, events, services and resources are provided to assist employees on their wellness journey. Comprehensive information regarding wellness for all UCR faculty and staff can be found on the Wellness website.

    • Employee-initiated Reduction in Time Program (ERIT)
