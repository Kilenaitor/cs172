The new R'Space faculty and staff portals are a revolution in information dissemination. These fully customizable pages place all your commonly used tools and resources at your fingertips. From Webmail to traffic and weather reports, to specialized web based applications, you select and access the content you want to see. At the same time, the portals will allow the University to let you know what is going on around our campus.
