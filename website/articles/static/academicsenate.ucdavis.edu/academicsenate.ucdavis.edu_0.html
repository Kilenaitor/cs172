Welcome to the Academic Senate

The Davis Division of the Academic Senate is composed of, and speaks as the voice of, UC Davis faculty. As defined by the Regents, the Senate establishes admission and degree policies, oversees courses and curricula, recommends faculty appointments and promotions, and advises on campus budgetary and administrative matters.
