Triton Innovation Challenge

The fifth annual Triton Innovation Challenge spotlights innovative, commercially promising, environmentally focused ideas that come from some of UC San Diego’s finest minds. Students, faculty and staff from across campus, including Scripps Institution of Oceanography, von Liebig Entrepreneurship Center at Jacobs School of Engineering, and Rady School of Management, compete for $11,000 in cash prizes to move their ideas forward.

Please join us for the TriNet Challenge event, where finalists the chance to present their project to the university community, as well as some of San Diego’s business and industry leaders.
