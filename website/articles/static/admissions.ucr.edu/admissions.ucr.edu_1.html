UCR BY THE NUMBERS

#1

university in the country based on graduation rates, affordability and the percentage of Pell Grant recipients. (Time Magazine)

19:1

Students to faculty ratio. Work closely with faculty!

#2

UCR's ranking based on contribution to the public good. (Washington Monthly)

4th

in student diversity among Forbes' ranking of America’s Top Colleges.

98%

3rd

best college for Hispanic students based on academics, tuition, a cultural center, degree programs and scholarships. (BestColleges.com)

in student community service participation and hours served. (Washington Monthly)
