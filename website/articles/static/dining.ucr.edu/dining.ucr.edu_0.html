Aberdeen-Inverness Residential Restaurant

 


Featuring Seven Food Stations


Aberdeen-Inverness Residential Restaurant offers continuous, all-you-care-to-eat dining in a relaxed, social environment. With seven food stations, find a wide-selection of classic and international, healthy and hearty cuisines. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options are offered daily.


MY GOURMET
Find your favorite global comfort dishes at My Gourmet, serving entrées such as meatloaf, turkey and mashed potatoes, hearty pastas, and other comfort dishes.

THE GRILL
Find grill classics such as fresh, never-frozen cheeseburgers, chicken sandwiches, hot dogs and traditional grill accompaniments. Vegetarian options are available.

WORLD'S FARE
To celebrate international cuisine, World’s Fare features dishes such as Greek gyros, pho, Asian teriyaki bowls, Chinese orange chicken and a continuous variety of the world’s tastiest meals. A section of World’s Fare always features a healthy entrée selection and vegan/vegetarian items.

DELI STATION
Create the ultimate sandwich at the Deli Station, from premium meats and cheeses, more than a dozen deli add-on options including everything from fresh tomatoes, assorted pickles, hummus, olives and more, to a variety of fresh breads.

SALAD BAR
Up to 21 items are available daily, including an assortment of salad greens, a multitude of vegetables, cut fruits, salad toppers, and a variety of dressings, including organic varieties.

REJUVENATION STATION
Rehydrate with tasty, feel-good beverages. The Rejuvenation Station offers infused waters, no-sugar added beverages, as well as fresh squeezed UCR orange juice as a healthy alternative to soda.

SWEETS & TREATS
Treat your sweet tooth. Our in-house bakery creates decadent desserts, including apple pies, double-chocolate fudge brownies and rich cakes. Gluten-free treats are available too.
