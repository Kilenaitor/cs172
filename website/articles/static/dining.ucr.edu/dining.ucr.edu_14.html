About the Market at Glen Mor

 

We’ve taken the best features of campus dining and put it under one roof at the Market at Glen Mor.

Find grocery offerings at Shop, homestyle meals to-go at Savor, fresh grill items at Sizzle, and your favorite coffee, lattes and Frappuccinos at Starbucks.

Take your food to go or enjoy outside dining that’s available year round on our temperature-controlled patio.
