Sandwich Platters, Giant Subs, and Desserts

Features legendary sandwiches, salads and more!

We offer complete catering packages, including sandwich platters, giant subs and individual "Subway to Go" boxed meals. For dessert, we offer platters of freshly baked cookies.

Frequently Asked Questions

 
  1. Submit the online Subway To Go catering request.
  2. Call the Subway on campus directly at (951) 827-4140.
  3. Visit our Subway location on campus and one of our management staff will assist you with your order.

A Subway manager will contact you via phone within two hours for orders placed during normal business hours. If order is received after hours, you will be contacted on the following morning.

No, simply walk up to the cashier and let them know you are picking up a catering order. They will notify the manager on duty to help you with the payment and pick up.

  • Cash
  • Department FAU
  • Debit/Credit Card (Visa/MasterCard)
  • Dining Dollars
  • Bear Bucks
  • Organizational Checks (Please note organizational checks require 5 business days for verification before any Subway To Go order can be picked up.)

We offer pick up service 7 days a week during our normal hours of operations. For pick up prior to opening hours on the weekends, please speak with one of or Subway management staff for arrangements.

Yes, we do offer a delivery service with a minimum purchase of $50.

Yes there is a fee for the delivery service. The fee will vary according to the amount of purchase. The following is the pricing structure:

  • $50 - $125 = $15.00
  • $126 - $250 = $30.00
  • $251 - $500 = $50.00
  • $501 and above will vary.

Delivery service is available during the following hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 10:00am – 5:00pm
  • Saturday: 10:00am – 2:00pm
  • Sunday: 10:00am – 5:00pm

Please contact a Subway manager for availability, if you wish to have your catering order delivered outside of the delivery service hours.

We try our best to provide service to all UCR locations and buildings on campus, however there are some locations we are unable to deliver to. Please contact a Subway manager at (951) 827-4140 to determine if your campus location is within our delivery radius.
