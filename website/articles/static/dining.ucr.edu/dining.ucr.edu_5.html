Citrus Grove Catering

 


From Simple to Sublime

From beverage service and continental breakfast to fun-filled barbecues and gala receptions, Citrus Grove Catering is committed to providing the finest in menu selections and service to the university community. Choose from a full-range of culinary choices and catering services to fit almost any budget.

 



 

Contact Us

 

Our experienced & professional Catering Team Members are prepared to offer you the personal attention required in all aspects of your gathering. It is our goal to provide the caliber of food and attention to detail that insures the success of every event planned at the University of California, Riverside.
