HUB Food Court

 


The HUB is the place to go for a variety of restaurants and plenty of seating.

Visit Habanero's for custom built tacos and burritos, or try La Fiamma for hearth-baked pizza, pastas and salads.

If you're in the mood for fresh-off-the-wok Chinese favorites, stop by Panda Express or grab fresh sushi at Sushi by Panda!

Habanero's

 


Built-to-Order

Get Mexican food just the way you want it at Habanero’s. Custom-build burritos, tacos, bowls or salads with your choice of grilled meat, beans, rice, cheese and salsa. Habanero’s is open for breakfast and lunch.

La Fiamma Italian Cuisine

 


Three Stations Dedicated to Italian Creations

La Fiamma Pizza

Offered by the slice, as a combo or the whole pie, get traditional hearth-baked Napolitano-style pizza. Enjoy the crisp crust and generous toppings. Along with classic cheese and pepperoni pizza, tasty sensations such as chipotle chicken and quattro formaggio are rotated daily. 

La Fiamma Pasta

Create deliciosa pasta dishes, choosing from our selection meats, vegetables, spices, and pastas topped with your choice of pasta sauces such as pomodoro, creamy Alfredo or a delightful combination of both. Your pasta creation is paired with warm, crusty bread sticks and one of our crisp Caesar side salads.

La Fiamma Salads

At La Fiamma Salads, we’re serious about salad. Get a freshly prepared Smoked Turkey Cobb, or try a Chilled Capellini Pasta Salad, with chilled capellini noodles tossed with toasted pine nuts, shredded mozzarella cheese, and diced tomatoes. Seasonal soups are available.
