UCR Farmer's Market
Every Thursday (excluding holidays)
UC Riverside now has it's very own weekly Farmers Market, held every Thursday from 4pm-8pm in Lot 14. Come by and shop for fresh produce, baked goods, natural products, and more. A variety of vendors will be selling each week. For more info, contact us at UCRFarmersMarket@ucr.edu.
