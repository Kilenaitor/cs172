Lothian Restaurants

 


Lothian Restaurants consists of two dining facilities within Lothian Residence Hall. Each serve fresh, high-quality meals and snacks throughout the day. Choose from Lothian Residential Restaurant for a variety of hot meals and healthy salads and Spinelli's for late night tasty brick oven pizza, chicken wings, and other options.

 

 

Lothian Residential Restaurant

 


Featuring Seven Food Stations

Lothian Residential Restaurant offers continuous, all-you-care-to-eat dining in a relaxed, social environment. Lothian’s seven food stations offer a wide selection of classic and international, healthy, and hearty cuisines. Vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free options are offered daily. Make sure to try Lothian's weekend brunch, featuring a variety of offerings such as rotisserie ham, roast beef, eggs Benedict, enchiladas, scrambled eggs, fresh fruit, country-style potatoes, sausage, pastries, biscuits and gravy, waffles and an omelet bar!





NEIGHBORHOOD GRILL
Serving rotating American diner classics, the Grill features grilled hamburgers, assorted sliders, melts, sandwiches, and all the sides and toppings plus French fries, onion rings and more. 

VEGETARIAN BAR
Always find irresistible dishes at the Vegetarian Bar, such as sloppy, veggie-Joe sliders, hearty soups, Polynesian soy chicken, garden teriyaki soy beef tips and more. 

GLOBAL SIZZLE
Our talented chefs prepare a variety of international-themed entrées that add a succulent flair to your plate, many customizable and a la minute.

URBAN KITCHEN
Urban Kitchen features traditional favorites such as our popular rotisserie chicken, pork loin, tri tip beef and salmon.

COMFORT TABLE
Oven-roasted and steamed vegetables, specialty salads, and sushi are rotated, along with an array of other delectable items at the Comfort Table.

VILLAGE GARDEN
Make your salad dream come true at Village Garden. With two serving stations, find a selection of salad greens, fresh-cut fruits, vegetables, nuts, salad toppings and dressings, along with assorted breads, rolls and smears.

CRAVINGS
Our in-house baker creates delicious treats daily, such as brownies, pies, cakes, and fresh gourmet cookies.












 

 

Savor Express

 


The same great home-style salads and entrees found at the Market at Glen Mor, now in a second convenient location!

Savor Express features a rotating menu that includes hot main entrées – such as slow-roasted chicken, pulled pork and BBQ ribs – alongside a selection of side dishes (hot and cold vegetable dishes, premium artisan salads, fruit and desserts). Use your Meal Swipe for a Combination Plate!
