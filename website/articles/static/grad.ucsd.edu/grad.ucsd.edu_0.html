Thank you for your interest in graduate study at UC San Diego. 

We offer a wide variety of academic and professional fields and we welcome talented prospective students from across the nation and around the world.  UCSD graduates have gone on to assume prominent roles in academia, industry, government, and the arts and media in California and beyond.
