Summer 2017 Session Dates:

Session A: June 26-July 29

UCR has a session for everyone!

The Summer Timeline contains important dates and deadlines which are subject to change without notice. Please click on the session your course is in and review these dates frequently to ensure that your classes are added/dropped within the allowed time frame.

UCR will be observing Independence Day on July 4th, and Labor Day on September 4th. There will be no instruction on these two days.


Please keep in mind when registering for UCR summer courses:
♦You are responsible for the grades and fees associated with each course in which you have enrolled.
♦It is your responsibility to drop any unwanted course(s) or section(s), including waitlist enrollments, prior to the published drop deadline(s).
♦To ensure enrollment in a course, you must pay for your fees in full by the end of the business day1 on the same day on which you enroll. Any balance due on your 'Summer Fees' screen that is not paid in full by the end of the business day1 will result in some or all of your classes being dropped. Click here for more information about the pay-as-you-go system.

1 Payments are accepted at the Cashier’s office during open hours; online payments can be made up to 9:00 PM (a non-refundable service fee is assessed for using online payment methods).
