The UCR Medallion is presented in recognition of extraordinary service, dedication and generosity to education at all levels and for enduring support of UC Riverside and the community.

Distinguished Alumnus Award
Byron Pollitt, Jr. ’73

Alumni Service Award

Outstanding Young Alumnus Award

For twenty-nine years, the UCR Alumni Association has honored graduates who personify the University's tradition of excellence and service. Through their personal and professional achievements, these individuals bring distinction to UCR, contribute to the betterment of society and enhance their communities.
