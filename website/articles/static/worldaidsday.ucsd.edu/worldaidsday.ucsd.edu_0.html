World AIDS Day 2016


The University of California, San Diego will honor World AIDS Day all week with a variety of free, public events, including a viewing of portions of the AIDS Memorial Quilt. World AIDS Day is honored every year on December 1st. This year’s events reflect the “Hands Up for HIV Prevention” theme, language symbolic of the global community effort needed to reinvigorate communities, repower #HIVprevention, and reduce infection rates. Please continue to check back for updated information.#ucsdAIDS16
