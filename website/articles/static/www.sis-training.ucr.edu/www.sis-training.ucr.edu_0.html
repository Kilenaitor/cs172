R'WEB SELF-SERVICE UPDATE

As you know, the campus has transitioned to R’Web
self-service. Some students have experienced challenges during registration.

We are aware of these challenges and are working diligently to resolve them as soon as is possible. We thank you for your patience during this time.

In the meantime, please encourage your students to continue registering or preparing to register for classes. If you or they encounter a problem, please report it immediately.

If students are unsure about how to register for classes using R’Web self-service, please have them follow these 
step-by-step instructions, or visit our FAQ’s page. They can also visit the Registrar’s website for detailed information on topics ranging from registering for classes, viewing the schedule of classes, obtaining Authorized User access, ordering transcripts, or preparing for graduation.

 

Welcome to Student Affairs Information Systems

Student Affairs Information Systems (SAIS) provides quality data and system support to Student Affairs and Enrollment Services. SAIS supports departments to recruit students, administer financial aid, and admit, enroll and retain an academically excellent and diverse student population. .
