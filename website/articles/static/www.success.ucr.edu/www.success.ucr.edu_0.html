The First Year in Student Affairs

Things can get pretty crazy here at UCR. If you're a typical first year student, you've got a lot going on. From your schoolwork and relationships to budgeting your time and money. From trying to plan your career to just trying to plan your day. It can all get pretty overwhelming.

Partner with a Student Mentor: 

Peer mentors help first year students like you with your transition to the university, help you feel more at home in the academic environment, and enhance your college experience. Go here to register!
