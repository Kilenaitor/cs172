Putting the U in UCR

\n

Whether you\'re a freshman, a transfer student or you\'re about to graduate, we\'re here to support you academically, socially and physically. We want to make it easy for you to take advantage of the endless opportunities at UCR to learn, contribute, get fit and have fun. It\'s all here.

\n
\n
    \n
  • Academic Resources We have created a list of resources and opportunities to help you stay on top of your education. Learn about the Academic Resource Center, available student resources, or look into UCR summer sessions and programs to study abroad.
    • \n
  • Campus Life Find out the weekly events and activities happening on campus throughout the school year.
    • \n
  • Commencement Find out about scheduling, participant information, ticketing and everything else you need to know about the annual event.
    • \n
  • Libraries UC Riverside\'s libraries are among the largest academic research libraries in the United States. Our collections hold over 2 million volumes, 28,000 print and electronic serial subscriptions, 1.7 million microforms, 300,000 government information sources, and access to 165,000 e-books.
    • \n
  • Services for Students Housing, child care, campus safety and everything else you need to be successful outside the classroom. There are a wide variety of services for students available at UCR.
    • \n
  • Transportation Need to buy a parking permit? Looking to join a carpool? How about looking up the trolley or bus schedule? Whether you commute or live locally, figure out your best option to get to campus.
    • \n
  • UCR on Facebook Connect with UC Riverside through Facebook to stay current with our community.
    • \n
  • Your Health, Fitness and Well-beingHealth services go beyond the doctor\'s office at UCR. Learn about Student Wellness Partners, a partnership of service centers on campus that are committed to helping you maintain physical, emotional and mental well-being.
    • \n
