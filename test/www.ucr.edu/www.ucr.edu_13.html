The UCR Alumni Association

\n

UCR provided you\nwith the tools necessary to succeed in today and tomorrow\'s world. By becoming a lifetime member of the UCR\nAlumni Association, you are continuing to build upon your strong\nrelationship with the campus.

\n

It is an exciting time to be a Highlander, as our\ncampus is thriving.
Don\'t miss out!\n

\nBecome a Member\n

\n

\n\t\t
\n\t\t\n\n\t\t\n\t\t
\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n

\n\n

UC Riverside alumni enjoy a lifetime of friendships and other benefits. The university takes pride in providing our alumni with a variety of continuing education and networking opportunities along with travel and entertainment options.

\n

There is something for every UCR alumni, from the members of the inaugural class to the most recent graduates. The campus is growing, changing and evolving, and we hope you will come home to visit.

\n
    \n
  • The UCR Alumni AssociationThe UCR Alumni Association preserves and promotes the relationships between alumni and UCR. Joining the association helps you to keep in touch with your classmates, connect with fellow UCR alumni and stay up to date on the campus as a whole. Visit the association website to learn more about becoming a member of the UCR Alumni Association.
    • \n
  • The Alumni & Visitors CenterThe Alumni & Visitors Center is the home of the UCR Alumni Association and a beautiful place for UCR friends and family to gather
    • \n

Legacy Brick Program

\n

For a tax-deductible gift of $500, you can purchase a 4x8 inch brick inscribed with a three-line, 20 character name or message of your choice that will be displayed in the entrance courtyard of the Alumni & Visitors Center. Learn more about the Legacy Brick Program.

\n

\n

Get Your Official Gear at the UCR Campus Store

\n

Show and share your Highlander pride by wearing official UC Riverside apparel from the UCR Campus Store. Can\'t make it to campus? You can purchase everything you need through our online store. UCRAA members can save 10% on clothing and emblematic items.

\n

Check store hours.
