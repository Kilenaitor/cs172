Partners in Your Student\'s Education

\n

At UC Riverside, you\'re a part of our family, too!

\n

The way we see it, we\'re partners in your child\'s education. It\'s our goal to keep you involved and informed as we provide your child with opportunities to succeed at the very highest level of higher education. We try to create a campus experience that is lively, diverse, challenging and safe.

\n

Planning a trip to UCR? Visit our About section to find information for visitors.

\n

Parents Association

\n

The UCR Parents Association allows you to stay involved in your children\'s education. Become a part of the educational experience by claiming your free membership with the UCR Parents Association.
