\n Read more about Spooky Music from the Tower

\n

The UCR Bell Tower 50th Anniversary carillon concert series includes a Bats in the Belfry Halloween Carillon Concert that will include themes from Ghostbusters, the Addams Family, the Munsters, and other haunted tunes. David Christensen, campus carillonneur, will play. Read more facts and figures about the tower in the UCR Magazine, including the fact that the tower has its own twitter feed.

\n Read more about The Search for the Arctic Bumblebee

\n

Writer Sean Nealon travels with a team of UCR scientists as they track Bombus polaris, an elusive bee that could teach us about climate change in the Arctic, one of the least understood environments on Earth. That story is on the cover of the Fall issue of the UCR magazine. Other stories include the 50th anniversaries of both the carillon tower and KUCR radio, the Olympic adventure of UCR alumna Brenda Martinez, and much more.
